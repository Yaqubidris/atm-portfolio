About Me

Abdullahi Tijjani Muhammad Gwarzo is a seasoned public servant and visionary leader, known for his dedication to sustainable development and grassroots empowerment. With a distinguished career spanning decades, he has held key political positions, including serving as the Deputy Governor of Kano State and Minister of State for Housing and Urban Development

Abdullahi Tijjani Muhammad Gwarzo was born on September 23, 1960, in Koyar Getso, Gwarzo Local Government Area of Kano State, Nigeria. A graduate of Ahmadu Bello University, he earned a degree in Textile Engineering and later pursued a distinguished career in public service.

Over the years, he has served in various leadership positions, from being Chairman of Gwarzo Local Government to Deputy Governor of Kano State (2003-2011). He played a vital role in the Greater Kano Water Project, earning him the nickname "Ruwa Baba." His service extended to the federal level as Minister of State for Housing and Urban Development (2023-2024).
